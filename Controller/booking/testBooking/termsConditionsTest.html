1. Booking Process

1.1

Bookings for the instructor's vehicle must be made at least two weeks in advance to ensure availability.

1.2

Any cancellations of the vehicle/instructor must be notified at least 48 hours before the scheduled test time to avoid a cancellation fee.

1.3

Driving tests are booked through the DVSA. The DVSA requires a minimum of 10 working days' notice to change or cancel a driving test. This rule is outside of our control. If you do not give the required notice, the DVSA will not issue a refund of the test fee.

1.4

If a test needs to be cancelled with the DVSA on your instructor's recommendation (e.g. if you are not test-ready), this must also be done at least 10 working days in advance to avoid losing the DVSA test fee.

1.5

You must present a valid provisional driving licence on the day of the test.

1.6

Alps Driving School and your instructor reserve the right to reschedule the test due to unforeseen circumstances, such as weather conditions, examiner availability, or vehicle issues beyond our control.
